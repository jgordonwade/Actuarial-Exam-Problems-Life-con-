\JGWitem{ LTAM May 2022 \#12}
Calculate the expected present value for a 10-year deferred annuity due paying five thousand per month issued to (55), using
an effective annual interest rate of 5\%, the Standard Ultimate Life Table, and the 2-term Woolhouse approximation. 
\showsol{\bsoln
\bears
     \ax**{x}[(m)] &\approx & \ax**{x} -  \dfrac{m-1}{2m} \\
     \ax**{65}[(12)] &\approx & \ax**{65} -  \dfrac{11}{24}  = 13.54979 - \dfrac{11}{24} = 13.09146 \\
     \ax**[10|]{55}[(12)] &=& \Ex[10]{55}\ax**{65}[(12)] = 0.59342\cdot 13.09146 = 7.76873 \\
     \text{\sc ans} &=& 12\cdot 5000\cdot 7.76873 = \boxed{466,124}
\eears
\esoln}

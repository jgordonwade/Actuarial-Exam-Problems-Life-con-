\JGWitem{ MLC Nov 2016, \# 2}   % \S2 Single Life Survival
You are given the following survival function of a newborn:
\[
  S_0(x) = \left\{\begin{array}{cc} 1-        \dfrac{x}{250}                & \mbox{ for } x\in[0,40), \\
                                                         1-\left(\dfrac{x}{100}\right)^2 & \mbox{ for } x\in[40,100].
 \end{array}\right.
\]
  Calculate the probability that (30) dies within the next 20 years.
\showsol{\bsoln
  \bears
     \prepostsub{20}{p}{30} &=& \dfrac{S_0(50)}{S_0(30)} = \dfrac{\frac{3}{4}}{\frac{220}{250}} = \frac{75}{88} \\
      \prepostsub{20}{q}{30}  &=& 1-\prepostsub{20}{p}{30} = 1-\frac{75}{88} = \frac{13}{88} = \underline{0.14772}
  \eears
\esoln}

 For an insurance:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Losses can be 100, 200 or 300 with respective probabilities 0.2, 0.2, and 0.6.
\item The insurance has an ordinary deductible of 150 per loss.
\item $Y^P$ is the claim payment per payment random variable.
\end{enumerate}
Calculate Var(Y P).

\item[(A)] 1500
\item[(B)] 1875
\item[(C)] 2250
\item[(D)] 2625

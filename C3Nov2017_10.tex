\showsol{\bsoln
If $P$ is the monthly gross premium then the Equivalence Principle gives:
\bears
  0 &=& \Expect{L_0^g} = \overbrace{100,000\Ax*{40}}^{\text{death ben.}} +  \overbrace{200+0.04\cdot 12P\ax**{40:\angl{20}}[(12)]}^{\text{expenses}} 
     - \overbrace{12P\ax**{40:\angl{20}}[(12)]}^{\text{premiums}}  \\ \\ 
   0 &=&100,000\Ax*{40} +  200 - 0.96\cdot 12P\ax**{40:\angl{20}}[(12)]
\eears
Under UDD,
\bears
    \Ax*{40} &=& \dfrac{i}{\delta}\Ax{40} = \dfrac{0.06}{\log(1.06)}\cdot 0.161324 = 0.166117 \\
    \ax**{40:\angl{20}}[(12)] &=& \ax**{40}[(12)] - \Ex[20]{40}\cdot \ax**{60}[12] \\
            &=& \Bigl(\alpha(12)\ax**{40} - \beta(12)\Bigr) - \Ex[20]{40}\cdot\Bigl(\alpha(12)\ax**{60} - \beta(12)\Bigr)\\ &=& 11.4247
\eears
so that
\bears
   0 &=&100,000\cdot 0.166117  +  200 - 0.96\cdot 12P\cdot 11.4247 \\
   && \boxed{P = 127.74}
\eears


\esoln}

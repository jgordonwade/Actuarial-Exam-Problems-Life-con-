\JGWitem{Nov 2001, \#25}
For a special fully continuous whole life insurance on (65):
\begin{enumerate}
\item The death benefit at time t is $b_t=1000e^{0.04t}, \; t\geq 0$
\item Level benefit premiums are payable for life.
\item $\mu_{65}(t)=0.02$ for $t\geq 0$
\item $\delta = 0.04$
\end{enumerate}
Calculate $\prepostsub{2}{V}{}$, the benefit reserve at the end of year 2.
\showMC{\begin{description}
\item[(A)] 0
\item[(B)] 29
\item[(C)] 37
\item[(D)] 61
\item[(E)] 83
\end{description}}
\showsol{\bsoln
We need the premium:
\bears
  0 &=& A - \pi\bar{a}_x \\
    &=& \left(\int_0^\infty v^tb_t f_{T_{65}}(t)\,dt \right) - \pi\bar{a}_x \\
    &=& \left(\int_0^\infty (e^{-0.04 t})(1000e^{0.04t})(0.02e^{-0.02t})\,dt \right) - \pi\bar{a}_x \\
    &=& 1000\left(\int_0^\infty 0.02e^{-0.02t}\,dt \right) - \frac{\pi}{0.02+0.04} \\
  \pi &=& 60
\eears

Now 
\bears
  \prepostsub{2}{V}{} &=& \left(\int_0^\infty (e^{-0.04  t})(1000e^{0.04{t+2}})(0.02e^{-0.02t})\,dt \right) - \frac{60}{0.06} \\
                      &=& 1000(e^{0.08}-1) = 83.2871
\eears
\esoln}

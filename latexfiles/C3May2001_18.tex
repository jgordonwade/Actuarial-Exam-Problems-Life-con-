\JGWitem{May 2001, \#18}
For a special fully discrete 20-year endowment insurance on (55):
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Death benefits in year k are given by $b_k = (21- k), \; k = 1, 2, \ldots, 20$
  \item The maturity benefit is 1.
  \item Annual benefit premiums are level.
  \item $\prepostsub{k}{V}{}$ denotes the benefit reserve at the end of year $k$ for $k = 1, 2,\ldots, 20.$
  \item $\prepostsub{10}{V}{} = 5.0$
  \item $\prepostsub{19}{V}{} = 0.6$
  \item $q_{65} = 0.10$
  \item i$ = 0.08$
\end{enumerate}
Calculate $\prepostsub{11}{V}{}.$
\showMC{\begin{description}
\item[(A)] 4.5
\item[(B)] 4.6
\item[(C)] 4.8
\item[(D)] 5.1
\item[(E)] 5.3
\end{description}}
\showsol{\bsoln
Prospectively, $\prepostsub{t}{V}{} =$APV future benefits - APV future premiums:
\[
   \prepostsub{19}{V}{} = v - \pi
\]
so $\pi = 1-\prepostsub{19}{V}{} = 0.3259$.

\medskip
\bears
   \prepostsub{10}{V}{} + \pi &=& vp_{10}(\prepostsub{11}{V}{} ) + vq_{10}b_{11} \\
   5.0 + 0.3259 &=& \frac{0.9}{1.08}(\prepostsub{11}{V}{} ) + \frac{0.1}{1.08}\cdot 10 \\
   \prepostsub{11}{V}{} &=& 5.28
\eears
\esoln}

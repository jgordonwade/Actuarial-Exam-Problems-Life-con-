\JGWitem{May 2001, \#25}
For a discrete probability distribution, you are given the recursion relation
\[p_k = \dfrac{2}{k}p_{k-1}\quad \text{for} k=1,2,3,\ldots. \]
Determine $p_4.$
\showsol{\bsoln
   This is an (a,b,0) recursion formula with $a=0$ and $b=2$  to the distribution is Poisson(2).
   So $p_k = \dfrac{e^{-2}2^4}{4!} = 0.09022$.

\esoln}

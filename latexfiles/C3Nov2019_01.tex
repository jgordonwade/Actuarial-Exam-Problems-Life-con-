\JGWitem{LTAM, Nov 2019, \#1}
You are given that $\px[t]{40}=\exp\left(-0.06\cdot (1.12^t-1)\right)$ for all $t\geq 0$. 
Calculate the probability that a life age 45 survives 10 years.
\showsol{\bsoln
  \bears
      \px[15]{40} = \px[5]{40}\cdot \px[10]{45} \quad\Longrightarrow\quad 
      \px[10]{45} &=& \dfrac{\px[15]{40}}{\px[5]{40}} \\
         &=& \dfrac{\exp\left(-0.06\cdot (1.12^{15}-1)\right)}{\exp\left(-0.06\cdot (1.12^{5}-1)\right)}  \\
         &=& \boxed{0.800376}
  \eears
\esoln}
